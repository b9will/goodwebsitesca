Local SEO for NDs

Why your city matters more than you think

When a patient searches for a naturopath, they're almost always searching with local intent: "naturopath Toronto," "naturopath near me," "naturopath open Saturday in Vancouver." These searches are won at the local level — not by having a generic naturopath website, but by having a website specifically optimized for your city and neighbourhood.

Google's local pack — the map results that appear at the top of local searches — shows only three results. Every site we build is optimized to compete for that map pack in your specific city.

What we do differently

Built for your market, not just your practice.

We don't just put your city name in a few headings and call it "local SEO." We build city-specific content, reference local landmarks and neighbourhoods where relevant, optimize your Google Business Profile for your specific service area, and structure your pages to rank for the exact search terms patients in your city use.

Every site we build includes local schema markup, a Google Business Profile setup or audit, and a local keyword strategy specific to the ND market in your city.
