Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Who we are

Good Websites is a web design subscription service for naturopathic practices, operated by B9 Digital Inc. ("we," "us," or "our"). Our website is goodwebsites.ca.

Questions about this policy can be directed to hello@goodwebsites.ca.

What information we collect

We collect information in the following ways:

Information you provide directly

  • Contact and booking inquiries — When you book a call or send us a message, we collect your name, email address, and any information you share in the message.
  • Client onboarding — If you become a client, we collect business information (practice name, address, services) and billing information necessary to provide our services.

Information collected automatically

  • Analytics — We use privacy-focused analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This may include pages visited, time on site, and general location (country/city level). We do not use Google Analytics.
  • Server logs — Our hosting provider may collect standard server log information including IP addresses, browser type, and pages requested.

How we use your information

We use information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and booking requests
  • Provide and improve our web design services
  • Send service-related communications (invoices, project updates)
  • Understand how our website is being used

We do not sell your information. We do not use your information for advertising purposes.

Third-party services

Our website and services use the following third-party services:

  • Calendly — Our booking page links to Calendly for scheduling. When you book a call through Calendly, their privacy policy applies to information you submit there.
  • Stripe — Client billing is processed through Stripe. Payment information is handled directly by Stripe and we do not store card details.
  • Fontshare — We use Fontshare to load web fonts. Fontshare may log requests for fonts served from their CDN.

Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels. We do not use cookies from Facebook, Google Ads, or other advertising platforms. Any cookies present are functional (session management) or come from the third-party services listed above.

Data retention

We retain contact inquiry data for up to 12 months. We retain client data for as long as the client relationship is active, and for up to 7 years after for accounting and legal compliance purposes.

Your rights

If you are located in Canada, you have rights under PIPEDA (Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act) including the right to access and correct personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@goodwebsites.ca.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information we collect. Our website is served over HTTPS. Access to client data is restricted to authorized personnel.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page with a revised "last updated" date. Continued use of our services after a policy change constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your information, please contact us at hello@goodwebsites.ca.
