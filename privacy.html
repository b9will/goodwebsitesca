Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Who we are

Good Websites is a web design subscription service for naturopathic practices, operated by B9 Digital Inc. ("we," "us," or "our"). Our website is goodwebsites.ca.

Questions about this policy can be directed to hello@goodwebsites.ca.

What information we collect

We collect information in the following ways:

Information you provide directly

  • Contact and booking inquiries — When you book a call or send us a message, we collect your name, email address, and any information you share in the message.
  • Newsletter subscription — When you subscribe to our newsletter using the form on this website, we collect your email address. Subscriptions are processed through Formspree (see Third-party services below). By subscribing, you consent to receiving our monthly newsletter covering website strategy, digital practice resources, and occasional information about Good Websites services. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • Resource downloads — When you request a downloadable resource (guide, checklist, template, or similar), we collect your email address to send it to you. By doing so, you consent to receiving the requested resource and related follow-up educational content. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • Client onboarding — If you become a client, we collect business information (practice name, address, services) and billing information necessary to provide our services.

Information collected automatically

  • Server and hosting logs — Our hosting provider (Cloudflare) may collect standard request log information including IP addresses, browser type, pages requested, and general geographic region. This information is used to operate and secure the website.

How we use your information

We use information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and booking requests
  • Deliver the newsletter and educational resources you have requested
  • Send follow-up educational content related to resources you have downloaded
  • Provide and improve our web design services
  • Send service-related communications to clients (invoices, project updates, edit requests)
  • Understand how our website is being used

We do not sell your information to third parties.

Email communications and consent

When you subscribe to our newsletter or request a resource, you are providing express consent under Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL) to receive electronic messages from Good Websites (B9 Digital Inc.).

Every email we send includes:

  • Our name and contact information
  • A clear description of why you are receiving the message
  • A working unsubscribe link

To unsubscribe at any time, use the unsubscribe link in any email we send or contact us at hello@goodwebsites.ca. We will process your unsubscribe request promptly.

We do not share your email address with other organizations for their marketing purposes.

Third-party services

Our website and services use the following third-party services:

  • Formspree — Our newsletter signup and contact forms are processed through Formspree. When you submit a form, your submission is handled by Formspree before being forwarded to us. Formspree's privacy policy applies to information processed through their platform. See formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.
  • Calendly — Our booking page links to Calendly for scheduling. When you book a call through Calendly, their privacy policy applies to information you submit there.
  • Stripe — Client billing is processed through Stripe. Payment information is handled directly by Stripe and we do not store card details.
  • Cloudflare — Our website is served through Cloudflare's network. Cloudflare may process request data as part of operating and protecting our website.
  • Fontshare — We use Fontshare to load web fonts. Fontshare may log requests for fonts served from their CDN.

Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels from Facebook, Google Ads, or other advertising platforms. Any cookies present are functional (such as remembering cookie consent preferences) or come from the third-party services listed above.

Data retention

  • Newsletter and email subscribers — We retain your email address for as long as you are subscribed. On unsubscribe, your address is removed from future sends. We may retain a suppression record to prevent accidental re-subscription.
  • Contact inquiries — Retained for up to 12 months.
  • Client data — Retained for as long as the client relationship is active, and for up to 7 years afterward for accounting and legal compliance purposes.

Your rights

If you are located in Canada, you have rights under PIPEDA (Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act) including the right to access, correct, and request deletion of personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@goodwebsites.ca.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information we collect. Our website is served over HTTPS. Access to client data is restricted to authorized personnel.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page with a revised "last updated" date. Material changes affecting how we use your information will be communicated by email to subscribers.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your information, please contact us at hello@goodwebsites.ca or by mail at:
