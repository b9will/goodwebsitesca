Questions about pricing

Honest answers.

Are there any setup fees?

No. The monthly price is the price. There are no setup fees, onboarding charges, or hidden costs. You pay the same amount every month.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes. Upgrade from Launch to Practice (or Practice to Authority) any time. We'll build out the additional pages and features. Downgrades happen at your next billing date.

What happens if I cancel?

Monthly plans cancel anytime with no penalty. Yearly plans run for the full year at the discounted rate. When you cancel, we export everything — your content, images, and copy — so nothing is lost.

Do I need to pay for my domain?

Your domain (e.g. drsmith.ca) is separate — you register and own it yourself, usually $15–20/year. Hosting is included in your plan. We'll handle connecting everything.

How does the yearly discount work?

Pay for 12 months upfront and get roughly 40% off the monthly rate. Launch drops to $59/mo ($708/yr), Practice to $119/mo ($1,428/yr), Authority to $209/mo ($2,508/yr). Billed as a single charge at the start of your year.

Is there a free trial?

No trial, but the first call is free and we'll show you a mockup of what we'd build before you commit. If it doesn't feel right, you owe us nothing.

Not sure which plan?

Book a free call. We'll look at your practice together and recommend the right fit. No pressure, no commitment.
