Our story

18 years on the web.

Every era changed the rules. We changed with them.

2007

Spider-Man 3
gave us this

WordPressHTML

2007

My first website was for a local business. I was in high school.

WordPress, raw HTML, a small business that needed Google. I had no idea what I was doing — but it ranked, and that clinic got more calls. That was enough to hook me for good.

2012

London Olympics
Usain Bolt ×3

AgencyCMSSEO

2012

Agency life. Beautiful work — for clients with big budgets.

I learned what great looked like. I also learned what it cost. The gap between what big clients got and what small practices could afford felt completely arbitrary.

2014

Ice Bucket
Challenge

SquarespaceTemplates

2014

Squarespace arrived and every site looked exactly the same.

Templates were a gift and a trap. Suddenly everyone had a website — and nobody ranked for anything because they were all identical.

2016

Pokémon GO
Summer 2016

WebflowNo-code

2016

Webflow. Page builders. No-code promised everything.

The tools got genuinely powerful. But nobody building them was thinking about the patient discovering a naturopath at 11pm who needed to trust someone before booking.

2019

Raptors NBA
Champions

Mobile-firstCore Web Vitals

2019

Google went mobile-first. A lot of practices went dark.

Slow on mobile? You dropped. NDs on outdated WordPress themes had no idea until new-patient bookings stopped.

2023

Cinema's
biggest weekend

ChatGPTAI SearchAEO

2023

ChatGPT started recommending practitioners. The window opened.

Practices with structured content and local markup got cited. The rest got ignored. The window to get ahead was open — briefly.
