Our journey

18 years building
the web.

2007

Before the iPhone. Every site was built for a 1024px screen.

Sites were designed for big monitors and mouse clicks. Mobile wasn't a concern yet.

WordPressFlashDesktop
2012

Responsive design landed. One URL, every screen size.

Mobile traffic exploded. Sites that didn't adapt got penalised in search. A new discipline was born overnight.

ResponsiveBootstrapMobile
2014

Wix. Squarespace. Suddenly anyone could have a website.

Templates lowered the barrier. "Good enough" became the goal. Most sites looked identical, and ranked accordingly.

SquarespaceWixTemplates
2016

Speed became a ranking signal. Slow sites started disappearing.

Google started penalising slow pages. Sites that had always been lazy about performance began paying the price.

PageSpeedUXConversion
2019

Google switched to mobile-first indexing. Desktop traffic kept falling.

If your site wasn't fast on a phone, it didn't rank. Most small-practice sites weren't close.

Mobile-firstCore Web VitalsLocal SEO
2023

AI started answering questions directly. Search changed overnight.

ChatGPT. Perplexity. Google AI Overviews. Sites structured for machines, not just humans, got cited. The rest didn't.

ChatGPTAI SearchPerplexity
2025

The bar is higher than it's ever been. We built a studio to clear it.

Fast, structured, credible sites at a price that makes sense for a solo practice.
