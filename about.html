Our time on the web

Since 2007.

2007

I built my first site before the iPhone existed.

Set up WordPress for a local small business. Everything was built for desktops: big screens, mouse clicks, Flash animations. That first project changed how I thought about building things.

WordPressSmall BusinessDesktop
2014

Built a Wix site for a friend's wedding. Fell down a creative rabbit hole.

Design. Photography. Video. Illustration. Every creative medium I touched taught me something new about communication. The web was becoming a canvas and I wanted to understand all of it.

WixDesignCreative
2016

The social media craze hit. I learned how to ride it.

Everyone was building an audience on Facebook and Instagram. I got into audio, podcasting, and content strategy, learning how people actually use the internet, not just how to build for it.

Social MediaPodcastingContent
2019

Went all in. Full-stack bootcamp: front-end, back-end, Node.js.

Built sites and brands with static HTML and CSS. Moved to WordPress for handoff ease, then Squarespace. Each platform taught me its limits. Every project sharpened the instinct for what actually works.

Node.jsStatic HTMLFull-stack
2020

Found Webflow. Built a startup's marketing site and app front-end.

More control than any template, less friction than custom code. Webflow clicked immediately. Solved problems across every system: Shopify, custom apps, integrations. Launched B9 Digital in 2021.

WebflowShopifyB9 Digital
2023

Started building Good Websites. AI arrived and changed the pace.

The idea had been building for a while. Then ChatGPT showed up and research that took days took hours. We pushed the concept forward faster than expected, moving quicker without cutting corners.

WebflowAIGood Websites
2025

The bar is higher than it's ever been. We built a studio to clear it.

Design, development, and business knowledge combined. Helping small businesses build a digital presence that actually works: fast, structured, and built to grow.
